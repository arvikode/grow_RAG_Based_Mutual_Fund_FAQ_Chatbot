**Source**: [HDFC Flexi Cap KIM](https://files.hdfcfund.com/s3fs-public/KIM/2025-11/KIM%20-%20HDFC%20Flexi%20Cap%20Fund%20dated%20November%2021,%202025_1.pdf)

### 2. Exit Load
